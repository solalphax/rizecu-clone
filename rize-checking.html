How Your APY Works

Reverse-Tiered Interest

Unlike traditional tiered accounts, we reward your first $10,000 with our highest rate.

$0 - $10,000 7.50% APY
Over $10,000 0.05% APY

Example Calculation

Balance: $15,000

  • First $10,000 earns 7.50% APY = $62.50/month
  • Remaining $5,000 earns 0.05% APY = $0.21/month
  • Total monthly dividend: $62.71

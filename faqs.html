Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

Rize Credit Union is a member-owned, not-for-profit financial cooperative serving members since 1952. We offer a full range of banking services including checking, savings, loans, and credit cards with better rates and fewer fees than traditional banks.

Membership is open to anyone who lives, works, worships, or attends school in Los Angeles County, Orange County, or San Bernardino County. Family members of current members are also eligible to join.

To earn 7.50% APY on balances up to $10,000, you must: 1) Make at least 5 debit card purchase transactions per statement cycle, 2) Be enrolled in eStatements, and 3) Open the account online. If requirements aren't met, you'll earn 0.05% APY on your entire balance.

Most accounts can be opened with $0. Share certificates (CDs) require a minimum deposit of $500. Business checking requires a $100 minimum opening deposit.

Many loan applications receive approval within minutes during business hours. Auto loans and personal loans can often be funded the same day. Mortgage loans typically take 30-45 days to close.
